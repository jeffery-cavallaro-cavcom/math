\subsection*{Problem}

Sketch one complete cycle of each of the following graphs.  Be sure to identify all key points, amplitude, and
vertical asymptotes (as is appropriate for each graph).
\begin{enumerate}
\item \(y=-5\sin(4\pi t+1)\)

    y=-5\sin\left[4\pi\left(t+\frac{1}{4\pi}\right)\right]
    A=5\ \text{(with vertical reflection)} \\
    \w=4\pi \\
    P=\frac{2\pi}{4\pi}=\frac{1}{2} \\
    b=\frac{1}{4\pi}\approx0.08\ \text{(left)} \\
    \text{key points at}\ x=-0.080,0.045,0.170,0.295,0.420
  \end{gather*}

  \begin{tikzpicture}
